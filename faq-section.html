Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Decanta.ai

What is Decanta.ai?

Decanta.ai is an AI sommelier platform for restaurants and wine enthusiasts. It provides expert wine pairing recommendations trained by Master Sommeliers, personal cellar management, bottle sharing technology, and a complete wine program management suite for restaurants and bars.

How does the AI sommelier work?

Decanta's AI sommelier is trained on Master Sommelier knowledge and provides instant wine recommendations based on food, occasion, taste preferences, and your personal cellar. Guests can access it via QR code at participating venues or through the Decanta.ai app.

Is Decanta.ai free to use?

Yes. Decanta.ai offers a free tier for wine enthusiasts that includes browsing the wine database, cellar management for up to 20 bottles, bottle sharing, and food pairing recommendations. Premium plans for enthusiasts and restaurants are also available.

How does bottle sharing work?

Decanta's smart bottle sharing lets wine lovers reserve glasses from premium bottles at participating restaurants. When enough people commit, the bottle is opened using wine preservation technology — giving access to exceptional wines by the glass without buying the whole bottle.

Can Decanta.ai help my restaurant increase wine revenue?

Yes. Restaurants using Decanta.ai have seen an average 30% increase in wine revenue. The platform provides AI sommelier service at every table via QR codes, smart bottle sharing to sell premium wines by the glass, inventory management, and a digital wine display.

How do I add my wine collection to Decanta.ai?

You can add wines to your cellar by scanning bottle labels with your camera, emailing wine invoices for automatic parsing, importing from a CSV or Excel file, or searching the global wine database. Your entire cellar can be digitised in minutes.
